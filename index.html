About Beatrice

She raced BMX.
Now she shoots it.

Beatrice Trang is a Toronto-based photographer whose background as a competitive BMX racer — she represented Canada at the 2017 UCI World Championships — gives her an instinct most photographers don't have. She's been in the action. She knows where to stand.

Her range spans concert photography (Dua Lipa, Benson Boone, Tate McRae), action sports, lifestyle, boudoir, and weddings. What threads it together: a clean eye, real presence, and a genuine feel for the people she photographs.
